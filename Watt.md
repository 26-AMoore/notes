# A watt is a unit of measurement for [[Power]], or useful energy at any given moment, also known as [[Energy]] per time

## Equation
$$W=P = VI$$
 P = [[Power]] / Watts
 V = [[Volts]]
 I = [[Ampere|Amps]]

Watt = 1 [[Joule|J]]/s
Kilo = $10^3$
Mega = $10^6$
Giga  = $10^9$
Tera = $10^12$
