## Math is the same as [[Mirror]]
Center of curvature
$$C=2f$$
Lens equation
$$\frac{1}{f}=\frac{1}{d_{o}}+\frac{1}{d_{i}}$$
Magnification
$$mag=\frac{d_{i}}{d_{o}}=-\frac{h_{i}}{h_{o}}$$
$\__{i}$ is image
$\__{o}$ is object
