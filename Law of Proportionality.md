#orbits #physics 
# One of [[Keplers Laws]]
### $$K=\frac{R^3}{T^2}$$
K = Keplers constant
R = Mean radius of [[Orbit]] (m)
T = [[Period]] of revolution (s)

# Two constants
$K_{sun} = 3.35*10^{18}$ ($m^3/s^2$)
$K_{earth} = 9.85*10^{12}$ ($m^3/s^2$)
