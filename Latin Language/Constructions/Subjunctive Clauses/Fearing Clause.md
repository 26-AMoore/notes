if your saying your afraid, its subjunctive

4 basic verbs
- timeo, timere -> I am afraid
- metuo, metuere -> I fear
- terreor, terreri -> I am terrified
- vereor, veriri -> I fear || I am afraid

Always use ne

to make
Fearing word in indicative + ne + subj verb

latin:
