# A relationship about the elongation of a spring compared to the [[Force]] applied to it 

$$F_{s}=-kx$$
$F_{s}$ = spring force ([[Newtons |N]]), always opposite $x$
$k$ = spring constant $\frac{N}{m}$, big number
$x$ = displacement (m)

#### [[Work]]
$$W_{s}=-\frac{1}{2}kx^2$$
#### [[Potential Energy]]
$$PE_{S}=\frac{1}{2}kx^2$$
